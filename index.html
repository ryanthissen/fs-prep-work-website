Early Life

I grew up in a small town in the California Central Valley. As a youth, I played soccer, baseball, and basketball. My first job was scooping ice cream at Baskin-Robbins. After high school, I attended Fresno State to study Civil Engineering.

College

While not studying, I was busy at my fraternity and going to Fresno State football games. I made many friends in college that will be with me my whole life. I was hired as a student at the California Department of Public Health to help staff engineers inspect public water systems.

Post-College

I worked three years in Sacramento following graduation. I then moved to Alameda where I would later meet my wife. Her work brought us first to Dublin, then back to the Central Valley. Then mid-2016, we moved back to the East Bay, where I eventually came across Galvanize.

Life

I'm married to a beautiful woman and we have a beautiful cat. I enjoy being outdoors, either walking in North Berkeley, SF, or Tilden Park. I enjoy fishing, golfing, going to Giants games, and exploring new restaurants.
